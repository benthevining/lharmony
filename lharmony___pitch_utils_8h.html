Converts a frequency in Hz to a MIDI note.

The formula is \( midi=69+12*\log_2\frac{freqHz}{440} \).

LHARM_EXPORT constexpr int limes::harmony::highestNoteOfMidiOctave ( int  octaveNumber)
constexprnoexcept

Returns the highest note of a given MIDI octave number (that octave's B key).

The formula is \( note=(O+1)*12+11 \). This formula puts 71 as the highest note of octave 4.

Here are the MIDI octaves, as defined by this formula:

Octave number Note number range Pitch range
0 12 - 23 C0 - B0
1 24 - 35 C1 - B1
2 36 - 47 C2 - B2

4 60 - 71 C4 - B4
5 72 - 83 C5 - B5
